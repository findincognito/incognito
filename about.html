About

Find Incognito

Find Incognito’s purpose is to be informative and active in the sharing of information on censorship globally, with interactive elements and core insight on five countries in particular. The site aims to simulate and give awareness into how people experience the internet in other countries. The United Kingdom being one of these five countries. The UK was selected due to the project being guided to those within the United Kingdom in particular. The UK is therefore a relatable viewpoint for most viewers of the site.

The remaining four countries include Russia, USA, Brazil, and Vietnam. They all fall under one or more of these categories:

• Harassment • State censorship • Disinformation • Spying surveillance
